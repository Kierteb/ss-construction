Our Services

From ground-up extensions to precision brickwork, we handle every stage of your project with care and skill.

Extensions

Whether it's a single-storey rear extension or a large wraparound, we design and build extensions that transform how you use your home. Our team handles everything from foundations and steelwork to plastering, electrics, and the final finish.

We've completed kitchen extensions, living space extensions, and conservatory conversions across South Essex, always on time and within budget.

Discuss Your Extension

Loft Conversions

Turn unused roof space into a bedroom, office, or bathroom. We manage the full build, including structural alterations, dormers, Velux windows, insulation, plastering, and staircase installation.

Every loft conversion is built to Building Regulations, with all sign-offs handled by our team.

Plan Your Loft Conversion

Garage Conversions

A garage conversion is one of the most cost-effective ways to add a room to your home. We convert single and double garages into kitchens, living rooms, home offices, and more.

We handle all structural work, damp-proofing, flooring, plastering, and electrics to create a space that feels like it was always part of the house.

Get a Garage Conversion Quote

Roofing

From flat roof replacements and fibreglass roofing to full re-tiles and leak repairs, we keep your home watertight. Our roofing work covers domestic and small commercial properties across Essex.

We use quality materials and proven methods to make sure every roof stands up to the British weather.

Book a Roof Inspection

New Builds and Outbuildings

We build garden rooms, home offices, workshops, and outbuildings from the ground up. Whether you need extra space for work, storage, or leisure, we'll design and construct a standalone structure that's built to last.

Our outbuildings are fully insulated, weatherproofed, and finished to the same standard as the main property.

Brickwork and Structural Work

Solid foundations start with skilled brickwork. We handle blockwork, brick walls, retaining walls, lintels, RSJs, and all structural alterations. Whether it's part of a larger project or a standalone job, we get it right first time.

From opening up load-bearing walls to building boundary walls, our structural work is precise and built to code.
