About SS Construction

A family-run building company rooted in South Essex, delivering quality work since day one.

Who We Are

SS Construction is a family-run general building company based in Grays, Essex. Founded by Mergim and his father Iskander, we've built our reputation on honest work, fair pricing, and results that speak for themselves.

We take on everything from kitchen extensions and loft conversions to full renovations and new builds. Every project, big or small, gets the same level of care and attention. We're not a faceless company with sales reps and call centres. When you hire us, you're working directly with the people who'll be on site building it.

We're proud to have earned 16 five-star reviews from homeowners across Essex and East London. Our clients come back to us and recommend us to their families, and that's the best measure of our work.

Based in Grays, we serve South Essex and the surrounding areas, including Thurrock, Basildon, Romford, Brentwood, and East London.
