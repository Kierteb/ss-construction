About SS Construction

A family building company rooted in South Essex, delivering quality work since day one.

Who we are

SS Construction is run by Mergim and his father Iskander. We are a family building company based in Grays, Essex, and we have been delivering honest, high-quality work for homeowners across the region.

We handle everything from rear extensions and loft conversions to full renovations and structural alterations. No job is passed off to subcontractors you have never met. When you hire us, you work directly with the people on site.

We are proud of our 16 five-star reviews. Our clients come back to us for their next project and recommend us to their families. That repeat trust says more than any advert could.

Based in Grays, we serve South Essex and the surrounding areas, including Thurrock, Basildon, Romford, Brentwood, and East London.
